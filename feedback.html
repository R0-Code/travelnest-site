FAQ

Frequently Asked

Is TravelNest free to use?

Yes — every feature on TravelNest is free. We're a discovery platform first.

Where is my data stored?

Your saved budgets, wishlist, and feedback live in your browser's localStorage — nothing leaves your device.

Can I install TravelNest as an app?

Absolutely. TravelNest is a Progressive Web App — add it to your home screen from your browser menu.

How are destinations chosen?

Our team curates each destination by hand, focusing on cultural depth, natural beauty, and traveler value.

Can I suggest a destination?

Yes — please use the form above and tell us where we should go next.
